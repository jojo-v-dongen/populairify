What is this website?

Have you ever wanted to explore new music?
Listen to songs that aren't chosen by Spotify's algorithms?
Maybe help a small music artist gain some more recognition?
That's great!
Because that is exactly what this website is designed to do.

How does it work?

After creating an account, you are able to listen and vote for unpopular songs that are randomly generated using the Spotify API. A 1-star vote indicates a strong dislike towards the song, while a 5-star vote indicates a strong enjoyment towards the song. Your vote will be anonymously saved in a databse to give the song an average rating and a chance to appear on the leaderboard.
